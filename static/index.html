About Us

People often say it's about the journey not the destination. But we at Mindscape disagree. From places inaccessible to a common man to the places with restricted access, We cover them all by providing live customised experience by connecting over video, with a trusted individual or entity from the experience you chose to take. Experience a plethora of Spaces and domains covering all the age groups aimed to learn from the practical and lively world experiences.



This platform will be open to many possibilities.For example, if you are a researcher and for a small part of it you need to connect to some place away from your location. Using our platform you will be able to connect to a trustworthy person from the location and then customize your live trip and do what you want to over video. And thus the possibilities are large
